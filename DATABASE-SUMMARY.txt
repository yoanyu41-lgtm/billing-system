    Purpose: Installment payment plans
    Key Fields:

    - product_id (FK → products.id)
    - total_price (decimal 10,2)

    - monthly_payment (decimal 10,2)
    - remaining_balance (decimal 10,2)
    - next_due_date (date, nullable)
    - status (enum: active/cancelled, default 'active')
    
    Contract fields:
    - contract_number (nullable)
    - contract_date (nullable)
    - witness_name (nullable)
    - witness_phone (nullable)
    
    - created_by (FK → users.id)
    - created_at, updated_at

    CALCULATION FORMULA:
    --------------------
    Principal = total_price - down_payment
    Monthly Interest = (Principal × interest_rate / 100) / 12
    Monthly Payment = (Principal / duration_months) + Monthly Interest
    
    EXAMPLE:
    Total Price: $1,000
    Down Payment: $300
    Interest Rate: 12% per year
    Duration: 10 months
    
    Principal = $700
    Monthly Interest = ($700 × 12 / 100) / 12 = $7/month
    Monthly Payment = ($700 / 10) + $7 = $77/month

================================================================================
                          BUSINESS LOGIC WORKFLOWS
================================================================================

WORKFLOW 1: CREATE INSTALLMENT PLAN
------------------------------------
1. Select/Create Customer
2. Run Credit Check (optional but recommended)
3. Add Guarantor Information (required for high-risk customers)
4. Select Product
5. Enter Payment Terms:
   - Total Price
   - Down Payment
   - Interest Rate (%)
   - Duration (months)
6. System Auto-Calculates:
   - Monthly Payment
   - Total Interest
   - Payment Schedule
7. Generate Contract Document
8. Create Installment Record
9. Send Telegram Notification to Customer

WORKFLOW 2: PROCESS PAYMENT
----------------------------
1. Customer Makes Payment
2. Staff Records Payment:
   - Select Installment
   - Enter Amount
   - Select Payment Method
   - Upload Payment Proof (if QR/bank transfer)
   - Set Payment Date
3. Payment Status: Pending
4. Admin/Manager Approves Payment
5. System Updates:
   - Remaining Balance
   - Next Due Date
   - Payment History
6. Generate Receipt
7. Send Confirmation via Telegram
8. Update Stock (if last payment - product delivered)

WORKFLOW 3: OVERDUE PAYMENT HANDLING
-------------------------------------
1. System Checks Daily for Due Payments
2. If Payment Missed:
   - Mark Installment Status: Overdue
   - Create Notification
   - Send Telegram Reminder
   - Log in telegram_logs
3. Staff Follow-up:
   - Contact Customer
   - Negotiate Payment
   - Record Notes
4. If Payment Received:
   - Process as Late Payment
   - Calculate Late Fees (if applicable)
   - Update Status back to Active

WORKFLOW 4: INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
---------------------------------
1. Receive Purchase Order:
   - Create Purchase Record
   - Add Purchase Items
   - Status: Pending
2. When Stock Arrives:
   - Mark Purchase: Received
   - Auto-Create Stock Movement (IN)
   - Update Product Stock Levels
3. On Sale:
   - Create Sale Record
   - Add Sale Items
   - Auto-Create Stock Movement (OUT)
   - Update Product Stock Levels
   - Check Min Stock Level
   - Alert if Reorder Needed

WORKFLOW 5: CUSTOMER CREDIT ASSESSMENT
---------------------------------------
1. Customer Applies for Installment
2. Staff Runs Credit Check:
   - Verify Employment
   - Verify Income
   - Calculate Debt Ratio
   - Review Payment History (if repeat customer)
3. Assign Risk Level:
   - Low Risk: High credit score, verified income, low debt
   - Medium Risk: Average score, some verification
   - High Risk: Low score, unverified, high debt
4. Decision:
   - Low Risk: Approve with standard terms
   - Medium Risk: Require guarantor
   - High Risk: Higher down payment or decline

================================================================================
                              STATUS DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

INSTALLMENT STATUS:
-------------------
• active: Currently ongoing, payments on track
• cancelled: Terminated before completion
• completed: All payments received (auto-set when balance = 0)
• overdue: Missed payment deadline

PAYMENT STATUS:
---------------
• pending: Payment recorded, awaiting admin approval
• approved: Payment confirmed and applied
• rejected: Payment declined (e.g., incorrect amount, fake proof)

PURCHASE STATUS:
----------------
• pending: Order placed, not yet received
• received: Stock delivered and added to inventory
• cancelled: Order cancelled

SALE STATUS:
------------
(Handled via completion - no explicit status field)

INVOICE STATUS:
---------------
• pending: Invoice issued, payment due
• paid: Payment received
• cancelled: Invoice voided

================================================================================
                           PAYMENT CALCULATION EXAMPLE
================================================================================

SCENARIO: Desktop PC Installment
---------------------------------
Product: Desktop PC Core i7
Total Price: $1,200
Down Payment: $400
Interest Rate: 10% per year
Duration: 12 months

CALCULATIONS:
-------------
1. Principal Amount:
   = Total Price - Down Payment
   = $1,200 - $400
   = $800

2. Monthly Interest Amount:
   = (Principal × Interest Rate / 100) / 12
   = ($800 × 10 / 100) / 12
   = $80 / 12
   = $6.67 per month

3. Monthly Payment:
   = (Principal / Duration) + Monthly Interest
   = ($800 / 12) + $6.67
   = $66.67 + $6.67
   = $73.34 per month

4. Total Interest Paid:
   = Monthly Interest × Duration
   = $6.67 × 12
   = $80.04

5. Total Amount Paid:
   = Down Payment + (Monthly Payment × Duration)
   = $400 + ($73.34 × 12)
   = $400 + $880.08
   = $1,280.08
